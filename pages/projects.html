Nmap Enumeration Lab

Objective: Identify open ports, running services, and potential attack surface on a target machine.

Tools Used

  • Nmap
  • Kali Linux

Step 1: Initial Scan

nmap -sS -T4 192.168.x.x

SYN scan used to quickly identify open ports. This gives a fast overview of exposed services.

Step 2: Service Detection

nmap -sV 192.168.x.x

Used to identify service versions. Important for finding known vulnerabilities.

Findings

  • Port 22 → SSH
  • Port 80 → HTTP

Analysis

HTTP service suggests potential web vulnerabilities. SSH may be targeted for brute force or misconfiguration.

What I Learned

Enumeration is the most critical phase. Without proper recon, exploitation becomes guesswork.
